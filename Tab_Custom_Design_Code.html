Our test uses a simple finger-prick blood sample to analyze your body's immune response to various foods through IgG antibody testing.
Blood tests measure actual immune responses (IgG antibodies), while hair tests lack scientific validation for food sensitivities. Blood tests provide more accurate and reliable results.
We only need a few drops of blood from a simple finger prick. Once we receive your sample, results are typically available within 5-7 business days.
When your digestive system is compromised, food particles can enter the bloodstream, triggering your immune system to produce IgG antibodies as a defensive response.
We analyze your blood sample for IgG antibodies against 200+ common foods. High antibody levels indicate potential food sensitivities that may be causing inflammation.
IgG food sensitivities can cause delayed reactions (up to 72 hours after eating), making them difficult to identify without testing. These reactions can contribute to chronic inflammation and various health issues.
